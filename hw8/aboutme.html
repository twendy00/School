About Me

Hi, I'm Wendy. I started learning to cook after college when I was living on my own and realized I couldn't subsist purely off of mac and cheese and PB&Js. As I started exploring beyond the pasta, bread and condiments aisles at the grocery store though, I quickly realized I had no idea what to buy and what was really considered healthy. After hemorrhaging more than enough money on unneccesary ingredients and snacks, I learned to meal plan in advance and arm myself with a grocery list before venturing to the store, and through enough time and experience, I've learned to be an expert meal planner.

However, as I learned to bring my lunches to work from my meal planning, I continually watched my colleagues spend money to purchase an unhealthy lunch every day. Why didn't they pack a lunch? Time and effort! My colleagues had a busy career and a family to raise, which made it hard to find the time to make healthy meals. Or, like I originally was, they just didn't know how.

As somebody who loves to create more time with friends and family by optimizing/automating work and chores, I decided to share the meal plans that I'm making every week to help others.

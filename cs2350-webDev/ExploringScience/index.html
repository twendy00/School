The Scientific Method

The scientific method is widely used in science. It consists of several steps. Depending on which list you look at, the steps might be different. However, if you consider carefully, it is apparent the steps are actually the same even when different words are used.

The process is also used in many other settings. Look at the steps in the scientific method and see how much you use this process.

The WISE Telescope

The WISE telescope was first launced in December 2009. It was used to take infared images of places too far away for us to see. The images are spectacular and available for all to see.

The WISE telescope was decommissioned February 2011 and put in hibernation for more than two years. It was reactivated under a new name in September 2013. Now the telescope travels under the name of NEOWISE.

Cats Breeds

Domestic Shorthair Cat

Cats are adorable. If you are interested in getting a cat, there are several breeds to choose from -- some of which are highlighted in the above slideshow. The most common cats are domestic shorthairs or longhairs. Essentially, the breeds of these cats are difficult to determine so they're given the generic name of domestic, similar to dogs being called "mutts".

If you decide to obtain a specific breed though, it's important to do your research and understand that specific breed's characteristics. For instance, Savannah cats are very beautiful and loyal. They were produced by cross-breeding a Siamese cat with an African Serval, a wild cat. The African Serval contributes to the Savannah's exotic look, but it will also have strong predatory instincts depending on how far removed it is from its wild ancestors. Due to their potential unpredictable temperament, some states have outlawed Savannah cats.

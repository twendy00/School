Juniper

The most playful and fiestiest cat ever!

Juniper being all cute and cuddly.

Here's a time line of Juniper's life:

  • December 2017 - Juniper was born.
  • June 2018 - Juniper found a mama to love and take care of her. Her mama loves her so much.
  • Summer 2018 - Juniper starts going outside. She loves exploring outside and catching bugs.
  • Winter 2018 - Juniper learns about winter and how cold it can be. She still wants to go outside though.
  • December 2018 - Juniper celebrates her birthday. Happy Birthday, Juniper!
  • January 2019 - Juniper gets a taste of what being home alone is like. She found that it gets lonely sometimes.
  • February 2019 - Juniper is spayed. No more babies for June Bug.
  • March 2019 - Juniper moves to a new home. It's calmer but smaller. She has mixed feelings.
  • April 2019 - Juniper acclimates to having a bigger family. She has a tuxedo kitty sister and human papa now.
  • July 2019 - Juniper eats 2 feet of string and has surgery. So scary! She was ultimately safe and sound though.
  • The Future - TBD....
